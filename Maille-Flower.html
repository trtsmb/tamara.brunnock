This tutorial is based on the flower video tutorial from TheRingLord.

    Materials:
  • 5 small scales
  • 11 Bright Aluminum 18 SWG 3/16"
  • 5 Bright Aluminum 18 SWG 1/4"
  • 5 Bright Aluminum 16 SWG 1/4"
  • 5 Bright Aluminum 19 SWG 5/32"
Step 1:
Open ten 18 SWG 3/16" rings. Attach two 18 SWG 3/16" rings to each of the five scales.

Step 2:
Attach the five petals to one 18 SWG 3/16" ring. The last two rings on the last petal will be a tight fit on the center ring.
Step 3a:
Attach an 18 SWG 1/4" ring through adjacent 3/16" rings on top of two petals. Do not place the ring through both rings on one petal. Continue all the way around.
Step 3b:
Turn the flower over. Attach a 16 SWG 1/4" ring through adjacent 3/16" rings on top of two petals. Do not place the ring through both rings on one petal. Continue all the way around.
Step 4:
Using the 19 SWG 5/32" rings connect the 18 SWG 1/4" rings to the 16 SWG 1/4" rings between the petals.
