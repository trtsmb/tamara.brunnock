This tutorial is based on the MailleArtisan's Celtic Labyrinth Unit.

    Materials:
  • 1 Bright Aluminum 18 SWG 9/32”
  • 10 Anodized Aluminum 18 SWG 3/16”
  • 20 Bright Aluminum 19 SWG 11/64”
  • 5 Bright Aluminum 20 AWG 9/64”
Step 1:
Place two closed 19 SWG 11/64” on two 18 SWG 3/16” AA and close both AA rings.
Step 2:
Place 5 groups of rings from step 1 on large 18 SWG 9/32” BA and close large ring.
Step 3:
Fold 2 silver rings back and add 2 19 SWG 11/64” rings like Byzantine weave. Do this for each of the arms.
Step 4:
Take 20 AWG 9/64” ring, catch silver & blue ring of one arm and blue & silver ring of adjacent arm. Continue around.
